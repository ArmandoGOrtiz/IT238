In addition to great food, we serve our customers better with state-of-the-art innovations that also benefit the environment. All of our kitchens operate with the most efficient equipment in the restaurant industry. We also strive to be green outside of the kitchen, too.

When we were constructing our Side Street and Other Boulevard location, home to our main kitchen and headquarters, we installed solar panels making us the first restaurant in the country to incorporate solar energy to directly heat our water. The panels allow for the use of a natural resource that is renewable, affordable and maintenance free. As you know, solar energy is also pollution-free, doesn’t cause global warming and, more importantly, reduces our dependence on foreign energy. We're also using energy-efficient tankless water heaters that help us waste less water and eliminate standby losses - energy that's wasted when hot water cools down in long pipes or while it's sitting in the storage tank.

At both our Main Street & and Side Street locations, we paved our parking lots with permeable pavers. Permeable pavers provide a solid ground surface and help the environment by allowing water, such as storm water or melted snow, to filter through the surface and drain into the underlying soil. This drainage keeps water pollutants out of our natural waterways, which protects the quality of our water supplies. Just another way we’re utilizing a natural resource and helping our environment!

We also have an extensive recycling program, use Energy Star appliances, energy-efficient lighting, radiant heat, high-efficiency HVAC units and we drive diesel fuel delivery trucks. Some of diesel fuel's environmental advantages are better mileage for diesel engines vs. traditional gasoline engines and that less refining is required for diesel fuel.

And new to our site, you can now order MRX gear featuring Hanes EcoSmart® shirts are made with up to 5% polyester created from recycled plastic bottles.

Wherever we can, we reduce, reuse, recycle, replenish and restore!
